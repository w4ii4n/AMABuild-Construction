modern minimalist kitchenette

Project Overview

A modern minimalist kitchenette featuring warm wood cabinetry, soft ambient LED lighting, and a clean neutral palette. The space includes sleek overhead cabinets with glass panels, open illuminated shelving, and a compact breakfast bar with a built-in wine rack design. Three elegant pendant lights hang from the ceiling accent structure, adding a cozy and luxurious atmosphere. The overall design blends functionality and contemporary aesthetics, creating a stylish yet inviting small-space kitchen interior.
